Works

Mismatch (2024)

Mismatch is a series of unique pen-plotted works created from a generative project coded in JavaScript. Each piece, displayed on A5 paper, features a distinctive composition of geometry and color arrangements. A selection of works can be viewed on Objkt. Click on any image for a larger view.

Moiré (2024)

Moiré is a series of works pen-plotted on paper. The first eight shown in the gallery below are in A4 format. The pieces explore the optical effects of moiré through various colors and geometric variations. Each output was generated using JavaScript. A selection of unique editions is exhibited on Objkt. Click on any image for a larger view.

Luz (2023)

Luz is a generative project coded in JavaScript. Composed of 200 editions, each output is unique, featuring a distinct grid composition and color palette. It was released in November 2023 on fx(hash).

The first work in the gallery below is artist-curated and was exhibited at the NFT Factory in Paris for the book launch Decentralization, Please Save Culture by New Society on November, 23 2023. It can be viewed on Objkt. Click on any image for a larger view.
